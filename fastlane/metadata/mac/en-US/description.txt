Create professional video thumbnails on your Mac without a complicated design workflow. Video Thumbnail Maker combines your photos into a crisp 1280 × 720 image using flexible grids, creative layouts, and precise controls.

Choose one to six photos, select a layout, and adjust the spacing, color, and divider angle. Auto Enhance brings out color and detail, while text tools help your title stand out. Save favorite settings for repeatable results and export the finished thumbnail directly to your Mac.

• Smart automatic layout selection
• Adjustable gap width, color, and angle
• Multiple Auto Enhance levels
• Text presets, positioning and visual effects
• Favorite settings for faster repeat workflows
• Standard 1280 × 720 widescreen output
• Private, local processing on your Mac
