Create professional YouTube thumbnails on your Mac without a complicated design workflow. YouTube Thumbnail Creator combines your photos into a crisp 1280 × 720 image using flexible grids, creative layouts and precise controls.

Choose one to nine photos, select a layout and adjust the spacing, colour and divider angle. Auto Enhance brings out colour and detail, while text tools help your title stand out. Save favourite settings for repeatable results and export the finished thumbnail directly to your Mac.
