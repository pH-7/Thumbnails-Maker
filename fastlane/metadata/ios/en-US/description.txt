Create scroll-stopping YouTube thumbnails right from your iPhone. Thumbnail Maker joins multiple photos into a single, perfectly sized 1280x720 image in seconds — no desktop, no design skills required.

Pick photos from your library, choose a grid layout, and tap Create. Adjust the spacing and gap colour, turn on Auto Enhance for a brighter, more vibrant look, then save straight to Photos or share anywhere.
